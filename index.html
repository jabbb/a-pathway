A PATHWAY

Starting in Upstate New York, USA, this channel of images leads across grass and dirt -- from continent to continent, computer to computer, pixel to pixel. Each "plot" was purchaced on Amazon Mechanical Turk for $0.10 USD from internet users all around the globe. Participants were instructed to take a picture of the ground such that it would visual match the previously posted image and thusly continue the path.

Feel free to take a scroll up and see where it leads.
